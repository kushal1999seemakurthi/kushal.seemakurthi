# Gemini Image Creation Prompts

Visual reference generation for ImmersivePort elemental portfolio scenes. Use these prompts with Gemini's image generation to create concept art and mood boards.

## Air Element Scene

**Prompt:**
```
Create a serene, minimalist illustration in the style of traditional East Asian ink painting (sumi-e). 
Show a large flying paper crane rendered in white and soft gray watercolor brushstrokes, 
drifting through layered clouds of varying opacity. The background features distant misty mountains in 
pale blue-gray, with a pale cream sky (hex #F5EFE0). Three horizontal cloud layers should be visible, 
each at a different depth. Include a barely visible calligrapher's ghost figure in the far distance, 
holding a brush. The overall mood is peaceful, welcoming, and contemplative. 
Aspect ratio: 16:9, high resolution, monochrome with cream accents.
```

## Water Element Scene

**Prompt:**
```
Create a stylized illustration of a teal river (hex #5FB3B3) flowing from left to right, 
rendered in East Asian ink painting style. Show a graceful koi fish swimming downstream, 
rendered in flowing watercolor brushstrokes. Along the riverbank, place three stone lanterns or markers, 
each glowing faintly with warm light. Include soft rain falling at the top of the image and 
caustic light patterns rippling on the riverbed below. The water should have depth and movement. 
Sky is overcast and cool-toned. Aspect ratio: 16:9, watercolor aesthetic, teal and cream color palette.
```

## Earth Element Scene

**Prompt:**
```
Create an illustration of two large ancient stone tablets emerging from the ground on either side of 
a tall central Shiv Ling-style pillar in a narrow canyon, rendered in East Asian ink style with ochre 
and brown tones (hex #C9854F). The central pillar rises high — its peak is NOT visible to the viewer, 
emphasizing depth beyond frame. The tablets have faintly glowing carved East Asian characters on their 
surfaces. Vines and crystalline formations thread through the stone seam cracks (callback to the water 
stage). Camera angle is low, looking slightly upward toward the tablets and the pillar. Canyon walls 
frame both sides with geological striations. Soft filtered light comes from above. 
The overall mood is grounded, stable, reverent, and ancient. Aspect ratio: 16:9, earthy ochre palette.
```

## Fire Element Scene

**Prompt:**
```
Create an ethereal illustration from the BACK-PERSPECTIVE (POV) of a Phoenix flying upward toward 
a rising sun. The viewer is positioned directly behind and slightly below the phoenix — only the 
partial wings and a small portion of the back of the head are visible at the BOTTOM of the frame. 
The creature is ascending toward a bright ember-orange sun (hex #E84F1F) centered in the upper 
area of the frame. Ethereal luminous clouds with warm golden tones fill the vast spacious sky above. 
Sparks and light trails rise like constellations around the path of ascent. The upper two-thirds of 
the frame is intentionally vast and open, with clouds and sky as the background — designed to accommodate 
project content text overlay. The overall mood is energetic, transcendent, ascending, and powerful. 
Aspect ratio: 16:9, warm orange/gold palette with luminous cloud atmosphere.
```

## Spirit Element Scene

**Prompt:**
```
Create a celestial illustration featuring a large, luminous ink seal (chop stamp) at the center, 
glowing in soft gold (hex #F2C76A). Around it in orbit, show four elements represented as small motifs: 
a cloud wisp (Air), a river fragment (Water), a stone shard (Earth), and an ember (Fire), 
each trailing luminous paths. An open journal floats below the seal with blank pages slowly filling 
with gold ink characters. In the background, a faint calligrapher figure from the opening walks into frame 
to place the seal. The mood is reflective, connective, and celebratory. Aspect ratio: 16:9, 
luminous gold and white palette with deep indigo background, mystical atmosphere.
```

## Transition: Ice Crystallization (Air → Water)

**Prompt:**
```
Create a dynamic transition illustration showing ink particles freezing into 6-fold ice crystal geometry, 
then fracturing into liquid drips. The camera should be positioned as if diving downward following 
a drip as it falls toward a still water surface. Ripples expand outward where the drip impacts. 
The color palette transitions from white-ivory (hex #F5EFE0) to teal (hex #5FB3B3). 
Show refraction of light through the ice and water. Aspect ratio: 16:9, 
fast-paced, transformative mood with magical crystalline effects.
```

## Transition: Bloom (Water → Earth)

**Prompt:**
```
Create an illustration of water saturating a riverbank, with reeds and tendrils sprouting and growing. 
Show the tendrils gradually hardening and thickening into bark, then petrifying into stone. 
A koi fish is swimming to the shore, coming to rest as stone wraps around it forming a tortoise shell. 
The camera view rises upward as if following a vine into a stone canyon. Color palette transitions 
from teal (hex #5FB3B3) to ochre (hex #C9854F). Mood: growth, transformation, grounding.
Aspect ratio: 16:9.
```

## Transition: Forge (Earth → Fire)

**Prompt:**
```
Create a dramatic illustration showing the transition from stone to fire in a pottery kiln setting 
(pots being fired for strength — earth is refined, not destroyed). A glowing crack opens in the ground, 
revealing fire beneath. On the left, ancient stone tablets and ochre earth tones. On the right, an 
active pottery kiln with glowing interior and active flames. The tortoise's shell glows and cracks open, 
releasing an AGILE SQUIRREL (small, nimble, full of life — NOT a phoenix yet). The squirrel leaps 
toward the kiln fire. Heat shimmer distortion is visible throughout. The camera is at ground-level, 
following fragments from left (cold stone) to right (hot kiln). Color palette transitions from ochre 
(hex #C9854F) to ember-orange (hex #E84F1F). Mood: acceleration, nervous energy of transformation, 
small-but-fast transitional creature. Aspect ratio: 16:9.
```

## Transition: Smoke to Ink (Fire → Spirit)

**Prompt:**
```
Create an ethereal illustration of sparks rising faster, with smoke thickening and spiraling upward. 
A phoenix flies upward into the smoke spiral and dissolves. The smoke condenses into liquid ink 
that flows into a central seal shape. The color palette transitions from ember-orange (hex #E84F1F) 
to luminous gold (hex #F2C76A). Light becomes cool and white, with calm settling over the scene. 
Mood: transcendence, completion, transformation into legacy. Aspect ratio: 16:9.
```

## Paper Crane (Creature Form 1)

**Prompt:**
```
Design a minimalist, low-poly paper crane suitable for 3D WebGL rendering. 
The crane should be rendered in white-ivory (hex #F5EFE0) with subtle shading. 
It should have graceful proportions with elegant wing curves and a long neck. 
The model should be approximately 2-3m in scale for a WebGL scene. Show the model from multiple angles 
(front, side, top). The style should be clean, geometric, and evocative of origami without being literal. 
Include wireframe view to show polygon count (target: <10k polys).
```

## Koi Fish (Creature Form 2)

**Prompt:**
```
Design a minimalist, low-poly koi fish suitable for 3D WebGL rendering. 
The koi should have flowing curves and elegant fins, rendered in teal (hex #5FB3B3) with subtle scaling details. 
Multiple viewing angles (front, side, top). The model should feel alive and graceful, swimming through water. 
Approximate scale: 2-3m for WebGL. Clean, geometric style with emphasis on fluid motion lines. 
Include wireframe showing polygon count (target: <12k polys). Show ripple effects around the fish.
```

## Agile Squirrel (Creature Form 4 — Transition Intermediate)

**Prompt:**
```
Design a minimalist, low-poly squirrel suitable for 3D WebGL rendering. The squirrel should convey 
agility, speed, and nervous energy — small but full of life. Rendered in warm amber-brown tones 
transitioning from ochre (hex #C9854F) to ember-orange (hex #E84F1F). The model should feel dynamic 
and mid-leap, with tail arched. Multiple viewing angles (front, side, top). 
This is a transitional creature between the slow tortoise and the soaring phoenix — it represents 
the burst of transformation energy before ignition. 
Include wireframe showing polygon count (target: <8k polys). Show the creature mid-leap.
```

## Usage Instructions

1. Copy the desired prompt verbatim into Gemini's image generation tool
2. Specify image dimensions: 1920×1080 (16:9) for maximum compatibility
3. Request multiple variations if the first attempt doesn't match the mood
4. Save high-resolution outputs as reference art in `public/design-references/`
5. Use the generated images to inform shader design and lighting setup in Three.js scenes

- **Water:** #5FB3B3 (teal)
- **Earth:** #C9854F (ochre)
- **Fire:** #E84F1F (ember-orange)
- **Spirit:** #F2C76A (luminous gold)
- **Canvas:** #F9F7F2 (cream background)
- **Text:** #1C1C1C (deep charcoal)
